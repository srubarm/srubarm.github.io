## Design Context

### Users
Czech-speaking tech enthusiasts and science readers interested in technology, health, and AI. They visit to read well-researched articles and expect a focused, distraction-free reading experience. The job to be done: understand complex technical and scientific topics clearly and quickly.

### Brand Personality
**Informative, Trustworthy, Technical.** The voice is that of a knowledgeable expert who explains complex topics with clarity and precision. Tone is confident but never condescending — authoritative yet accessible.

### Emotional Goals
Readers should feel **confidence and clarity** — they leave understanding the topic better than when they arrived. The interface should reinforce trust through clean structure and thoughtful presentation.

### Aesthetic Direction
**Clean modern editorial** — minimal whitespace-driven layouts with sharp typography and strong visual hierarchy, blending the readability of Medium with the editorial quality of Wired. The current blue palette (#4183C4 links, #47629A headings, #8ea5d1 accent bar) with green category tags (#4CAF50) forms the color foundation. Avoid visual clutter, excessive decoration, or playful/casual aesthetics.

### Design System
- **Theme:** Customized Reverie (Jekyll)
- **Colors:** Blues (#4183C4, #47629A, #8ea5d1), green accents (#4CAF50), neutral grays (#404040 body, #222 dark, #eee borders), lavender blockquotes (#ede1ff)
- **Typography:** System font stack + Roboto, 18px base, 1.7 line-height, Helvetica Neue for headers
- **Layout:** 740px max-width, 640px mobile breakpoint
- **Code blocks:** Dracula theme on dark (#282a36)

### Design Principles
1. **Clarity over cleverness** — Every design choice should make content easier to understand. If it doesn't aid comprehension, remove it.
2. **Trust through consistency** — Uniform spacing, predictable patterns, and systematic use of color build reader confidence.
3. **Content is king** — The interface serves the writing. Typography, whitespace, and hierarchy exist to make articles shine.
4. **Accessible by default** — Reasonable contrast, readable font sizes, and sensible structure so all readers can engage with the content.
5. **Bilingual-aware** — Design must accommodate both Czech and English content gracefully without layout breaking.
